# Formula 1 2022: Pit Stop Performmance & Strategy Analysis

**OVERVIEW**
This project analyzes pit stop performance across the 2022 formula 1 season, focusing on speed, efficiency and strategy timing.
The dashboard was built using power BI and explores how teams and drivers perform during pit stops,a critical component of race strategy

**OBJECTIVES**
-Identify the fastest pit stop teams
-Analyze pit stop consistency across drivers
-Understanding pit stop timing strategies
-Evaluate distribution of pit stop performance

**KEY INSIGHTS**
-Top teams consistently achieve pit stops under 2.5 seconds
-Majority of pit stops fall within the 2.5-3.5 second range
-Pit stop timing varies significantly depending on race strategy
-Some extreme pit times indicate technical issues or penalties

**TOOLS USED**
-Power BI
-Data Cleaning (Power Query)
-DAX for KPI calculations
